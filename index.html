About Noor-Class

NoorClass is an educational platform that connects students with qualified Quran, Arabic, and academic teachers — from the comfort of their homes.

We believe every student deserves access to high-quality, values-driven education — no matter where they live. Our mission is to make personalized Islamic and academic learning accessible, engaging, and meaningful for students worldwide.
